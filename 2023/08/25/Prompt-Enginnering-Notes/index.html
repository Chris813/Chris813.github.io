What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt Engineering, also known as In-Context Prompting, refers to methods for how to communicate with LLM to steer its behavior for desired outcomes without updating the model weights.

Guidelines

Principle 1: Write clear and specific instructions

Tactic(战术) 1: Use delimiters to clearly indicate distinct parts of the input

  • Delimiters(分隔符) can be anything like: ```, “””, < >,<tag> </tag>,:

Tactic(战术) 2: Ask for a structured output

Tactic(战术) 3: Ask the model to check whether conditions are satisfied

Tactic(战术) 4: “Few-shot” prompting

Give successful examples of completing tasks .Then ask model to perform the task.

Principle 2: Give the model time to “think”

Tactic(战术) 1: Specify the steps required to complete a task

Tactic(战术) 2: Instruct the model to work out its own solution before rushing to a conclusion

Model Limitations: Hallucinations

LLMs make statements that sound plausible but are not true.

Reducing hallucinations:

First find relevant information, then answer the question based on the relevant information.
